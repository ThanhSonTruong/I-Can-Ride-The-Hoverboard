INSTRUCTIONS:

Use the Arrow keys or keys IJKL to move around.

Up/I: Accelerate towards the head of the figure.

Down/K: Accelerate away from the head towards the board.

Left/J: Rotate counter clockwise.

Right/L: Rotate clockwise.

Shoot laser beams with the X key and slow

down time by pressing Z on the keyboard.

Beat the enemy to get points and win the game. P to Pause and M to Mute.
